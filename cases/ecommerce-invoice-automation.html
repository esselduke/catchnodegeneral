Automation E-commerce

E-commerce company reduces invoice processing from 3 days to 4 hours

How we automated invoice extraction, validation, routing, and posting for an online retailer managing 300+ supplier invoices monthly, eliminating manual data entry and reducing errors by 95%.

The Challenge

An online retailer selling consumer electronics was growing rapidlyâ€”monthly revenue had tripled in 18 months. But their accounts payable process hadn't scaled. Two AP clerks were drowning in paperwork, manually processing 300+ supplier invoices every month.

The process was entirely manual: Open email attachments, read PDF invoices, type data into QuickBooks, match to purchase orders, route for approval, and eventually process payment. Each invoice took 15-20 minutes of focused work. With 300 invoices monthly, that's 75-100 hours of pure data entry.

Critical Pain Points

  • Invoices arriving via email in various formats (PDF, image scans, Word docs)
  • Manual data entry leading to typos and transposed numbers
  • Delayed processing causing late payment fees and strained vendor relationships
  • No visibility into pending invoices or approval status
  • AP team working overtime every month-end close
  • Difficulty scalingâ€”couldn't afford to hire another AP clerk

The CFO calculated they were losing $8,000 annually to late payment penalties alone. More concerning: manual errors were causing duplicate payments and missing early payment discounts worth 2% (roughly $48K annually on $2.4M in supplier payments).

Our Solution

We designed an end-to-end invoice automation system that handles everything from email receipt to QuickBooks posting, requiring human intervention only for exceptions and final approval.

Automation Workflow

01

Intelligent Document Capture

Power Automate monitors dedicated AP inbox, extracts attachments, and uses AI Builder's invoice processing model to extract key fields (vendor, amount, date, line items, tax) with 98% accuracy.

02

Automated Validation & Matching

System validates extracted data against business rules, matches invoices to purchase orders in their ERP, flags discrepancies, and checks for duplicates automatically.

03

Smart Routing & Approval

Invoices automatically route to appropriate managers based on amount thresholds and department. Managers approve via email or mobile Teams app with one click.

04

QuickBooks Integration & Posting

Once approved, invoice data posts directly to QuickBooks with proper coding, attaches PDF copy to transaction, and schedules payment based on terms to capture early payment discounts.

Exception Handling

  • Low-confidence extractions flagged for manual review
  • PO mismatches sent to purchasing team for resolution
  • Duplicate invoice warnings prevent double payments
  • Dashboard shows all pending approvals and exceptions
