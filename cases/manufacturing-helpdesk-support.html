IT Support Manufacturing

Manufacturing company reduces IT downtime by 75%

How 24/7 helpdesk support with 15-minute response times eliminated costly production delays caused by technical issues, saving over $180K annually in lost productivity.

Industry: Manufacturing
Company Size: 150 employees
Timeline: Ongoing support

The Challenge

A precision manufacturing company operating two 12-hour shifts (6 AM - 6 PM and 6 PM - 6 AM) was losing significant revenue to IT-related production stoppages. Their internal IT person worked Monday-Friday, 8 AM - 5 PM, leaving second shift and weekends completely unsupported.

When technical issues occurred outside business hours—printer failures, network outages, MRP system crashes, or workstation problems—production workers had no recourse. They'd leave sticky notes on the IT person's desk and wait until morning. Meanwhile, production lines sat idle.

Critical Pain Points

  • No IT support for second shift (6 PM - 6 AM) or weekends
  • Average 12 hours per month of production downtime due to IT issues
  • Single IT person overwhelmed and unable to maintain systems proactively
  • Aging servers and network equipment failing frequently
  • No documentation of systems, passwords, or configurations
  • Production managers frustrated by recurring preventable issues

The financial impact was severe. At $2,000 per hour of production downtime (labor, overhead, and delayed orders), 12 hours monthly meant $288K annually in lost productivity. The Operations VP calculated that even a 50% reduction would justify significant IT investment.

Hiring two additional IT staff to provide coverage would cost $200K+ annually in salary and benefits. They needed a more cost-effective solution that provided enterprise-level support without enterprise-level overhead.

Our Solution

We implemented a comprehensive managed IT support program combining 24/7 helpdesk coverage, proactive system monitoring, and quarterly on-site maintenance to prevent issues before they impact production.

Support Implementation

01

24/7 Helpdesk with Guaranteed Response

Established dedicated phone line and ticketing system with 15-minute response time SLA. Employees call or text for immediate support from Tier 1 and Tier 2 technicians covering all shifts and weekends.

02

System Documentation & Knowledge Transfer

Documented all systems, created network diagrams, cataloged software licenses, and organized passwords in secure vault. Established procedures for common issues enabling faster resolution.

03

Proactive Monitoring & Maintenance

Deployed monitoring tools tracking servers, network equipment, and critical workstations. Automated alerts notify our team of issues before users notice. Monthly patch management and quarterly on-site preventive maintenance.

04

Infrastructure Upgrades & Modernization

Replaced failing equipment, upgraded network infrastructure for redundancy, implemented backup internet connection, and migrated critical systems to more reliable platforms with failover capabilities.

Service Level Commitments

  • 15-minute initial response time, 24/7/365
  • 4-hour resolution for critical production-impacting issues
  • Next business day resolution for non-critical tickets
  • Monthly service review with IT dashboard and recommendations
  • Quarterly on-site visits for preventive maintenance

Results & Business Impact

The transformation was immediate. Within the first month, second-shift workers resolved three critical issues that previously would have stopped production until morning. Over the following year, the compound benefits of proactive monitoring, rapid response, and preventive maintenance dramatically reduced unplanned downtime.

Downtime Reduction

12 hrs → 3 hrs

Monthly IT-related downtime dropped 75%. Proactive monitoring catches issues before production impact. Second shift no longer waits until morning for support.

Cost Savings

$180K/year

Avoided production losses totaling $180K annually. Service cost $72K/year—delivering 2.5x ROI from downtime reduction alone, excluding other operational benefits.

Response Time

Avg 11 minutes

Average response time of 11 minutes beats 15-minute SLA. 94% of critical issues resolved within 4 hours. Zero overnight production stoppages in last 6 months.

User Satisfaction

4.9/5 stars

Employees rate support quality 4.9/5. Production managers report IT is no longer a concern. Internal IT person now focuses on strategic projects instead of firefighting.

"Before Catchnode, IT problems meant production stopped until morning. Now when second shift has an issue, they call the helpdesk and get immediate support. We've eliminated millions in potential revenue loss. The service pays for itself many times over."

Tom Richards VP of Operations

Additional Operational Benefits

Beyond downtime reduction, the comprehensive support model delivered unexpected advantages:

  • Strategic IT Planning: Internal IT person shifted from reactive firefighting to strategic initiatives like ERP optimization and automation projects
  • Vendor Management: We handle all vendor relationships (ISP, software vendors, hardware suppliers), saving management time and ensuring accountability
  • Security Improvements: Proactive patching and monitoring significantly improved security posture, preventing ransomware incident that hit a competitor
  • Documentation Value: Complete system documentation enables faster training and reduces risk of knowledge loss
  • Budget Predictability: Fixed monthly cost replaced unpredictable emergency IT expenses

6-Month Performance Metrics

  • 487 helpdesk tickets resolved (average resolution time: 2.3 hours)
  • 23 proactive alerts prevented potential outages
  • Zero unplanned server downtime since infrastructure upgrades
  • 100% patch compliance on critical systems
  • 4 weekend emergency calls, all resolved within SLA
