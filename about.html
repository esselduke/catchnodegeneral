We help businesses move faster and work smarter

Catchnode was founded on a simple belief: technology should empower people, not frustrate them. We're a team of IT professionals, automation specialists, and security experts dedicated to making business technology actually work.

Our Mission

To eliminate the friction between businesses and their technology. We believe every company—regardless of size—deserves reliable IT support, intelligent automation, and robust security without the complexity or cost of building an internal IT department.

Our Approach

We're outcome-driven, not ticket-driven. Instead of just fixing problems as they arise, we proactively identify opportunities to automate, secure, and optimize. Every engagement starts with understanding your business goals, not just your technical requirements.

Our Values

The principles that guide every client engagement and internal decision.

Clarity Over Complexity

We explain technical concepts in plain language. No jargon, no gatekeeping. If we can't explain it simply, we don't understand it well enough.

Client Success First

We succeed when our clients succeed. Every recommendation is made with the client's best interest in mind, even if it means less revenue for us.

Proactive Problem-Solving

We don't wait for things to break. We monitor, anticipate, and fix issues before they impact your business. Prevention beats reaction every time.

Continuous Improvement

Technology evolves, and so do we. We're constantly learning new tools, techniques, and best practices to deliver better results for our clients.

Our Journey

2019

Founded

Catchnode was founded by a team of IT professionals frustrated with the traditional managed services model. We saw too many companies paying for reactive support when they needed proactive partnership.

2020

First 50 Clients

During the remote work transition, we helped 50 businesses rapidly deploy cloud infrastructure, implement security protocols, and maintain business continuity without skipping a beat.

2022

Automation Focus

We expanded our automation practice after seeing the massive impact of even simple workflow automations. Clients were saving 20-40 hours per month, and we knew we could do more.

2024

200+ Clients & Growing

Today, we support over 200 businesses ranging from startups to established enterprises. We've automated thousands of hours of manual work and secured millions of sensitive records.
