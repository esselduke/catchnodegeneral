Cloud & Cybersecurity

Protect your data and ensure business continuity with comprehensive cloud security services. From secure migrations and encrypted backups to multi-factor authentication and endpoint protection—we implement enterprise-grade security without enterprise complexity.

Get Started View Pricing
The Problem

Your data is at risk

Without proper security measures, your business data is vulnerable to ransomware, data loss, unauthorized access, and compliance violations. Many companies don't realize they're exposed until it's too late—and recovery is expensive, time-consuming, and reputation-damaging.

Our Solution

Layered security that just works

We implement multiple security layers—encrypted backups, multi-factor authentication, email security, endpoint protection, and secure cloud storage. Our approach is practical and cost-effective, giving you enterprise-level protection without requiring a dedicated security team.

The Outcome

Sleep better with protected data

Your business data is secure, backed up, and recoverable. Employees use strong authentication. Email attacks are blocked. You're protected against ransomware and data loss. Most importantly, you have peace of mind knowing your systems are secure.

Comprehensive security services

We protect your business with proven security practices that are practical, affordable, and effective—no security degree required.

Cloud Storage Migrations

Seamless migration to OneDrive, Google Drive, or other cloud platforms with zero data loss. We handle permissions, folder structures, and user training.

Automated Backup Systems

Set-and-forget encrypted backups that run automatically. Your data is protected with version history and rapid recovery options.

Password Manager Deployment

Enterprise password managers (1Password, Bitwarden) that generate and store strong passwords securely. No more sticky notes or reused passwords.

Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)

Add an extra security layer with MFA/2FA across all critical systems. Even if passwords are compromised, accounts stay secure.

Email Authentication Setup

SPF, DKIM, and DMARC configuration to prevent email spoofing and improve deliverability. Protect your domain reputation and stop phishing attacks.

Endpoint Protection

Monitoring and protection for all devices accessing your business data. Detect threats, enforce security policies, and respond to incidents quickly.

Security Awareness Training

Educate your team on security best practices with training sessions and simulated phishing campaigns. Your employees become your first line of defense.

Compliance Support

Guidance for SOC 2, HIPAA, GDPR, and other compliance requirements. We help document security controls and prepare for audits.

How we've secured businesses like yours

Real security implementations that protected data, prevented breaches, and gave business owners peace of mind.

01

Complete Security Overhaul for Growing Startup

A 50-person SaaS startup had minimal security—shared passwords, no backups, no MFA. We implemented a password manager, enabled MFA across all systems, set up automated encrypted backups, configured email authentication (SPF/DKIM/DMARC), and deployed endpoint protection. Total implementation time: 3 weeks.

Impact: Zero security incidents in 18 months, passed first SOC 2 audit, clients report increased trust
02

Cloud Migration with Zero Downtime

A professional services firm needed to migrate 15TB of data from an aging file server to OneDrive for Business. We planned the migration in phases, maintained folder permissions, trained users on the new system, and completed the transition over a weekend with no disruption to business operations.

Impact: 15TB migrated successfully, 100% data integrity verified, reduced IT costs by $2,400/year
03

Ransomware Recovery & Prevention

After a ransomware attack encrypted a client's files, we restored their data from our automated backup system (they had onboarded 3 months prior), then implemented layered security: endpoint protection, email filtering, MFA, and security awareness training with phishing simulations. They've remained attack-free since.

Impact: Full data recovery in 4 hours, $50K+ ransom avoided, no subsequent security incidents
